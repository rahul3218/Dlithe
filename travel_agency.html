Destinations

Bangalore

Experience the vibrant city life, lush gardens, and modern technology hub. Bangalore, known as the "Silicon Valley of India," also offers attractions like Lalbagh Botanical Garden and Cubbon Park.

Taj Mahal, Agra

Experience the beauty of one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, this white marble mausoleum is a symbol of love and an architectural masterpiece.

Goa

Enjoy stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich Portuguese heritage. Goa is famous for its picturesque coastline, seafood, and thrilling water sports.

Jaipur, Rajasthan

Explore the royal heritage and majestic forts of the Pink City. Visit the Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and Amber Fort to experience the grandeur of Rajput architecture.

Kerala

Experience the serene backwaters, lush green landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. Kerala is known for its houseboat cruises, ayurvedic treatments, and delicious cuisine.

Varanasi

Witness the spiritual aura and the ghats of the Ganges. Varanasi, one of the oldest cities in the world, is a major pilgrimage site with mesmerizing Ganga Aarti ceremonies.

Mysore

Explore the royal heritage and magnificent palaces. Mysore is famous for the grand Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill, and the vibrant Dasara festival.
